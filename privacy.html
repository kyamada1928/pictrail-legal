PicTrail Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

PicTrail is an iOS app developed by Koki Yamada. PicTrail helps users view photos with location information on a map.

Information We Use

PicTrail may access your photo library to display photos that contain location metadata on a map.

PicTrail may access your current location when you choose to move the map to your current area.

How Information Is Used

Photo library information is used only to display photos on the map. Photo location metadata is used only for map display. Current location is used only to move the map near your current location.

On-Device Processing

Photos, photo metadata, and location information are processed on your device. PicTrail does not upload your photos to a server. PicTrail does not send photo location information to a server. PicTrail does not store photo files outside your device.

Full Scan

When the full scan feature is used, PicTrail stores only the metadata needed for map display on your device. Photo files themselves are not copied, uploaded, or externally stored.

Third Parties

The current version of PicTrail does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, login services, or server synchronization.

The current version of PicTrail does not share photos, photo location information, current location, or photo metadata with third parties.

Future Advertising

PicTrail may introduce ads in a future version. If advertising SDKs are introduced, this Privacy Policy and App Store privacy information will be updated.

PicTrail will not provide photos, photo location metadata, current location, or photo metadata to advertising SDKs for ad targeting.
