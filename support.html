Frequently Asked Questions

Why are some photos not shown on the map?

PicTrail can show photos that contain location information. Photos without location metadata may not appear on the map.

Why do I see only some photos?

If you selected Limited Photo Access in iOS, PicTrail can only access the photos you selected. You can change photo access permissions in the iOS Settings app.

Does PicTrail upload my photos?

No. PicTrail processes photos and location metadata on your device. Photos are not uploaded to a server.

What is Full Scan?

Full Scan checks your photo library for photos with location information and stores only the metadata needed for map display on your device. Photo files themselves are not copied or uploaded.
